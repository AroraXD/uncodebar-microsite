uncodebar 2023

Sat, 9th September | 10:00 - 17:30 BST

Now in the NINTH year, uncodebar, the codebar unconference is back! Our annual one-day conference that encourages the codebar community to come together, share knowledge, and strengthen the community that supports our workshops.

At an unconference:

- no topics predetermined

- no keynote speakers

- no panels or working groups

Instead:

+ collaboration

+ no agenda until the day

+ freedom & discussion
